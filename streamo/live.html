  • SUNDAY SEERVICE
  • WEEKDAY SERVICE
  • Director: Wisdom Center Church
  • Sermon By: Pastor Googz Moffat

I want to talk to you about the subject “Just Enough Faith”,
That is a phrase I have been hearing a lot in my personal prayer time and study time. And if I am honest, when I think about having just enough faith, it makes me feel a little bit less than, like perhaps I am failing because whether we try to admit it or not, there are moments where we don’t have this abundant faith or we don’t have this super faith that everyone else is having, I know what it is like to have just enough faith.
Sometimes I have just enough faith to get me through today, I don’t know what is going to happen this week or next month or next year and if I start to think about it I start getting stressed out and I start to feel afraid but right now in this moment I have just enough faith.

Prayer: Lord save me from my doubts, fears, and insecurity, help me to recognize that I am not out here alone in the storm and no matter where I am in this journey I am just a reach away from your grasp. Show me how to endure to the end. Save me Lord not just from the storm but save me from my own doubt.
