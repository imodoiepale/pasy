AUGUST MESSAGE  

2 COR 4:13 TPT We have the same spirit of faith that is described in the scriptures when it says, ‘first I believed, then I spoke in faith. So we also believe then speak in faith.

We have the same spirit of faith- we don’t have a different spirit. We have the same identical Holy Ghost that was in the Lord Jesus and Apostle Paul and the rest of the Apostles. So we can say I have the same Faith that the Apostles have therefore I can produce the same results they did! Peter’s shadow healed cripples I can too. It is the same faith.

Secondly we also are supposed to be speaking. What are we to speak in faith? That which is written in the scriptures about God, the believer, and what we can do and who we are in Him. We say what God says in His word concerning us and circumstances-sin-trials-faith-etc. There are 5 things to do with the word. FIND IT

  • FIND WHERE IT IS WRITTEN AND MEDITATE ON IT
  • BELIEVE IT
  • RECEIVE / ACCEPT/ENDORSE WHAT THE WORD PROMISES BY FAITH BEFORE IT PHYSICALLY MANIFESTS
  • SPEAK IT/SAY IT SHOUT IT-SING IT-VERBALIZE IT
  • ACT ON IT-UNITE IT WITH A CORRESPONDING ACTION-HOLD ON TO THE WORD YOU BELIEVED AND CONFESSED DESPITE OF ANY CONTRADICTING REPORT UNTILL IT MANIFESTS DON’T DOUBT!



THE SEQUENCE TO FOLLOW IS THIS.

FIRST FIND IT IN THE SCRIPTURES –WHAT DOES THE BIBLE SAY?

We are responsible for reading and studying the bible. If we want to know who God is what the bible says, then we must personally cultivate the culture of studying it. The word of God carries ALL the solutions we are looking for. The word of God is God himself talking. The word of God is eternal-living and active-alive and powerful. But it won’t jump from the pages of the bible into your heart! So read! Joshua was told the secret of success, the word! The Holy Spirit revealed to David how to prosper in everything you do: the word!

Jos 1:1
Now after the death of Moses the servant of the LORD it came to pass, that the LORD spake unto Joshua the son of Nun, Moses' minister, saying,
2
Moses my servant is dead; now therefore arise, go over this Jordan, thou, and all this people, unto the land which I do give to them, even to the children of Israel.
Jos 1:5
There shall not any man be able to stand before thee all the days of thy life: as I was with Moses, so I will be with thee: I will not fail thee, nor forsake thee.
6
Be strong and of a good courage: for unto this people shalt thou divide for an inheritance the land, which I sware unto their fathers to give them. {unto this...: or, thou shalt cause this people to inherit the land, etc}
7
Only be thou strong and very courageous, that thou mayest observe to do according to all the law, which Moses my servant commanded thee: turn not from it to the right hand or to the left, that thou mayest prosper whithersoever thou goest. {prosper: or, do wisely}
8
This book of the law shall not depart out of thy mouth; but thou shalt meditate therein day and night, that thou mayest observe to do according to all that is written therein: for then thou shalt make thy way prosperous, and then thou shalt have good success. {have...: or, do wisely}

Ps 1:1
Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful. {ungodly: or, wicked}
2
But his delight is in the law of the LORD; and in his law doth he meditate day and night.
3
And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that bringeth forth his fruit in his season; his leaf also shall not wither; and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper. {wither: Heb. fade}

Jesus found where it was written of him. He read it and said what the portion of scripture said about him. He owned what was written in the word about Him by the prophet and boldly confessed it. Do you know what the word says about you? Sin? the devil? Grace? God’s Mercy-Kindness-Peace-authority-submission-giving-blessings-divine ability-favor etc.? If you don’t know then you can’t BELIEVE. You can only believe that which you have evidence of. God’s word is evidence. Find out what is written. Jesus defeated Satan through the written word he knew. He said to every attempt by Satan to tempt him. It is written. Jesus was therefore a student of the Bible-He was studious.
