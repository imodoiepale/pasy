THE POWER OF OUR WORDS  

God loves when we praise him. Praising God is responding verbally to what the word says. It is saying the same thing that the word of God says about God, who you are as the believer, what you can do in Christ Jesus, what you have in Christ Jesus, etc. Speaking contrary to what the word says is considered unbelief. It is complaining and murmuring and that will cause your life stagnate or circumstances to get worse.

You want to receive help from God then praise him. You want the power of God to be released on your behalf then praise him, God is willing to help you if you agree with him. Praise activates His presence. God dwells-stays-lives as in a house-in an environment of praise. His looking for one who needs his help on the earth. However, what qualifies one to be helped is his faith in God and his confession

2Ch 16:9 For the eyes of the LORD run to and fro throughout the whole earth, to shew himself strong in the behalf of them whose heart is perfect toward him. Herein thou hast done foolishly: therefore from henceforth thou shalt have wars. {to shew...: or, strongly to hold with them, etc}

Start today to watch what you say. Your words determine what happens in your life. Your words determine what good comes to you. If you speak God’s words, then you will see His power manifest.

Jesus said you shall have-[posses-own] that which you say. If You speak lack-defeat-poverty-and sickness you will experience exactly what you say

Mr 11:23 For verily I say unto you, That whosoever shall say unto this mountain, Be thou removed, and be thou cast into the sea; and shall not doubt in his heart, but shall believe that those things which he saith shall come to pass; he shall have whatsoever he saith.

Before you speak that word of God you MUST know it. To know the word, you have to read it and store it in your heart first. You cannot say what you don’t know. The reason why many are defeated is because they don’t know that the words they speak create realities over their lives. From today watch your mouth. Say only what God says about your life-business-finances-health-children-future etc. That is how to cooperate with God.

NEVER SAY AM SICK

You are called to heal the sick not look for healing therefore if you get sick take authority nad heal that sickness from your body using the name of Jesus!
Mr 16:17 And these signs shall follow them that believe; In my name shall they cast out devils; they shall speak with new tongues;
Mr 16:18 They shall take up serpents; and if they drink any deadly thing, it shall not hurt them; they shall lay hands on the sick, and they shall recover

  • Isa 33:24 And the inhabitant shall not say, I am sick: the people that dwell therein shall be forgiven their iniquity.
  • 1Pe 2:24 Who his own self bare our sins in his own body on the tree, that we, being dead to sins, should live unto righteousness: by whose stripes ye were healed. {on: or, to}
  • 3Jo 1:2 Beloved, I wish above all things that thou mayest prosper and be in health, even as thy soul prospereth. {wish: or, pray}
  • Ex 15:26 And said, If thou wilt diligently hearken to the voice of the LORD thy God, and wilt do that which is right in his sight, and wilt give ear to his commandments, and keep all his statutes, I will put none of these diseases upon thee, which I have brought upon the Egyptians: for I am the LORD that healeth thee
