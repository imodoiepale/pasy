DON,T GIVE UP  

Heb 10:23 Let us hold fast profession of our faith without wavering; (for he is faithful that promised;)

In life you will face different kinds of challenging situations some of which can be very overwhelming to go through. However, we have an assurance that The Lord Jesus is always with us and that He conquered Satan and his demons for us. Therefore, we are advised NOT to fear or allow doubt to dissuade us from the truth of God’s word but to be bold and confident especially when facing challenges.

John 16:33..In the world ye shall have tribulation: but be of good cheer; I have overcome the world.

What do you do when you face challenges? Cry? Leave church? Change churches? Give up on God? Get mad at everyone else? Feel sorry for yourself? Go hiding for a while? Give everyone a silent treatment? Blame people for your challenges? Well majority of people give up and allow the trials they go through to gain an upper hand over them and ultimately dictate their moods, that should NOT happen to a child of God who knows the word of God.

We are not to give up and we are not of those that lose. The bible says we are more than victors-and that victory is through the Christ in us.

Romans 8:37 Nay, in all these things we are more than conquerors through him that loved us.

1 John 4:4 Ye are of God, little children, and have overcome them: because greater is he that is in you, than he that is in the world.

1 John 5:4 Na for whatsoever is born of God overcometh the world: and this is the victory that overcometh the world, even our faith.

Faith in times of trouble will cause you to ride above any challenge or trial. That faith must be based on God’s promises. You need to know what the word says and take the word and speak it against the challenges’. You can’t keep quiet and win trials, to win you must take the word and speak to the challenges and maintain your victory.

Speak to your trials -command the storms of life to cease and calm down using the word of God-remember you shall have what you say

Mark 11:23For verily I say unto you, That whosoever shall SAY unto this mountain, Be thou removed, and be thou cast into the sea; and shall not doubt in his heart, but shall believe that those things which he SAITH shall come to pass; he shall have whatsoever he saith.

  • When the doctor says you are not going to be blind, you take the word and say who you are in Christ and what you have in you-that is [life in your body]-begin to speak to the condition command it to dry-dissolve or be rooted out -speak health and strength. this is how to win
  • The other thing to do is rejoice. Keep rejoicing not at the trial but at the fact that you are more than a victor and that Christ said He will never leave you nor forsake You-Don’t allow at any moment the condition to dictate how your day will be or how your countenance looks no! Let the word of God be continually your meditation and let your mind be fixed only on what the word says not the trial.
  • Every time you feel or are reminded of the problem, shout our hallelujah and say what the word says about you that’s what the opening verse instructs us to do. Hold fast-[tightly-without giving up-wavering] your profession-[confessions of faith] because God is faithful that promised. The trial may not seem to respond at first but that doesn’t matter keep speaking the word! God is faithful!

DECLARATION

I am a victor. I am blessed beyond measure and no weapon fashioned against me shall prosper in Jesus name! I live above every challenge and all Satan’s demons- I am reigning in life and dominating in every circumstance by the power of the holy spirit that lives in me. Victory has been granted me-victory is mine-halleluyah!
